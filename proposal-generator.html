05 What We Need From You

One video advertisement, 30 seconds or less, in any modern format (MP4, MOV). Your video can include your business name and logo, a special community offer, contact details, and a call to action.

If you don't have an existing video, a well-designed slideshow with your logo, offer, and contact details set to music works perfectly and can be produced at minimal cost.
